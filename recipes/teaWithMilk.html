Tea With Milk

Description

When I was in middle school, before milk tea was a thing, not many people drank tea with milk. One of my classmates' mom opened a little shop that sold tea with milk. I tried theirs and did not like it. So I cooked my mine milk tea. They are simpler and unlike modern milk tea.

Ingredients

  • Two Black Tea
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk

Steps

  1. Boil the tea with hot water in a pot.
  2. Put milk, sugar, and sweetened condensed milk in.
  3. Boil with medium heat for another 5 to 8 minutes.
  4. Cool in fridge when finished. Enjoy with your favorite snacks.
