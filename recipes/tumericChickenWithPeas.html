Tumeric Chicken With Peas

Description

I used to make these during Covid and it was easy to make and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Peas(frozen or fresh)
  • Chicken thigh
  • Tumeric
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Warm your pan or wok with oil, thaw peas if frozen, and cut thigh into small bite pieces.
  2. Put chicken in, medium to high heat and fry a bit until its color changes.
  3. Put the spices, water, and peas in
  4. Boil with medium heat, make sure to cover the pan or wok.
  5. When water is almost gone, your food is finished. Enjoy with rice.
