Eggs with Tomatoes

Description

Eggs with Tomatoes is one of the easiest and also tastiest Chinese meal.

Ingredients

  • Two Tomatoes
  • Two Eggs
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Ketchup (optional)

Steps

  1. Warm your pan or wok with medium heat.
  2. Mix the eggs until a moderately even mixture is produced.
  3. Wash and cut the tomatoes into small pieces.
  4. First put eggs in, then tomatoes, mix, crush, or chop them so that they are fully mixed together.
  5. Put in water, salt, and optional ketchup and continue mixing everything until eggs and tomatoes are cooked.
  6. Voila! Your Eggs with Tomatoes is done. You can decorate it or just enjoy it with rice.
